About Me

I am a junior Computing major born and raised in Austin, Texas. Growing up, I had an early fascination with computers and technology, as my father is a software engineer and always had brought some of his work home with him. As a kid, when my teachers asked me what I wanted to be when I grew up, I always said I wanted to be an inventor, although I can honestly say I had no idea what that meant at the time. When I reached high school, I ended up taking a computer science class and immediately began to take an interest in the field. Simultaneously, I also discovered photoshop and began to take another interest within the field of Graphic Design. This ultimately culminated in my current studies at Texas A&M, which are computer science and graphic design, as my major allows me to place emphasis on my art minor.
